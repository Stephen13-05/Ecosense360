Carbon Footprint

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases, primarily carbon dioxide (CO₂), that are emitted directly or indirectly by an individual, organization, event, or product. It is usually measured in metric tons of CO₂ equivalent (CO₂e) over a specific time period.
